Kidolive Syrup for Immunity is a dietary supplement with beta-glucan, zinc and standardized black elder fruit extract. Beta-glucan is a natural polysaccharide found in the cell walls of microorganisms from the Saccharomyces cerevisiae species. Thanks to a special method of Yestimun® beta-glucan extraction, this product contains as much as 85% of pure beta-1,3/1,6-glucan, which is free of any ingredients that could affect its action (above all, proteins and fats).
